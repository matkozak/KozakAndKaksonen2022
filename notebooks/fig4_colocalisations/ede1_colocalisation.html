Experiment

Rationale

The aim of the experiment was to determine whether mCherry-tagged endocytic proteins co-localize with Ede1-EGFP marked droplets in apl3âˆ† yap1801âˆ† yap1802âˆ† yeast cells.

Imaging and analysis

Two-color single planes were acquired on the Olympus IX81 equipped with a 100x/1.49 objective.

Measuring colocalization by correlation did not prove not very useful when applied to entire cells, since all endocytic proteins are correlated in intensity in major regions (background, cytoplasm and genuine endocytic patches).

I decided that a manual assessment would be more trustworthy. Regions of interest containing Ede1-EGFP droplets were cropped out of both channel images based only on the examination of the GFP channel. I used the ImageJ macro blindAnalysis to sequentially display all images at random, blinding me to the strain of origin, and recording a binary decision about colocalization for each image.
